What You'll Cover
  • Wrist preparation and shoulder mobility drills for safe, sustainable practice
  • Wall-assisted progressions — chest-to-wall, back-to-wall, and heel pulls
  • Body alignment and line: stacking shoulders, hips, and ankles
  • Kick-up technique and entry methods
  • Balance corrections — finger pressure, weight shifting, and micro-adjustments
  • Freestanding handstand holds and building volume over time
  • How to structure your own handstand practice going forward
Who It's For
Beginners — No handstand experience needed. We start from scratch.
Intermediate — Can hold a wall handstand and want to work toward freestanding.
